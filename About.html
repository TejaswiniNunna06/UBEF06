Utilisation of Electricity by farmers

Farmers extensively utilize electricity in various aspects of their operations. They employ electric pumps for irrigation, powering lighting systems for poultry and dairy farming, and operating machinery like milking machines, feed mills, and grain dryers. Electricity also powers heating and cooling systems in greenhouses, automated farming equipment like drones and sensors, and farm lighting for extended working hours. Moreover, electricity is used for water treatment, farm ventilation, and even electric fencing for livestock management. By leveraging electricity, farmers significantly enhance their productivity, reduce labor costs, and improve overall farm efficiency.
